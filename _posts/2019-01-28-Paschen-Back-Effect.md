# Hamiltonian

The total Hamiltonian $$H$$ of an atomic system in a magnetic field is given by

\\[H=H_0+H_B\\]

\\[H_B=\frac{e_0h}{4\pi mc}(\vec{L}+2\vec{S})\cdot\vec{B}+\frac{e_0^2}{8mc^2}(\vec{B}\times\vec{r})^2\\]
